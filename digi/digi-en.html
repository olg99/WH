Digital support

at Welcome House

Sweden is a highly digitalized society

Many parts of Swedish society are digital today.

It is important to be able to use digital tools to have full access to the society's services.

At Welcome House, we can show you how and help you learn to use the internet, a smartphone, or a computer.

Want to improve your digital skills?

We have various activities where you can learn to use different digital tools.

You can use your mobile or borrow one of our computers or tablets.

You can take a group course, or we can meet individually.

Welcome to the City of Stockholm!

Coming to a new country can be challenging, and that's why we are here to support you.

Our responsibility is to ensure that your initial time in Stockholm goes well.

Assistance in arranging the essentials

Once you have obtained your residence permit, there are many things that need to be arranged.

During your initial period in Stockholm, a social guide can assist you in:

  • applying for a personal identification number, bank account, ID card
  • enrolling children in school and preschool
  • undergoing a health examination and accessing healthcare
  • registering with the Employment Service (Arbetsf√∂rmedlingen)
  • understanding how society functions and learning more about Stockholm

For those who need extra support

Intro Stockholm's Support Team offers you additional counseling and assistance.
We guide you in communication with other authorities.

You have the right to apply for temporary financial aid until you can support yourself.
Intro Stockholm's Social Workers assess the eligibility for financial assistance for you and your family.

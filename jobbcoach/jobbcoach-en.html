Support in job search

at Welcome House

At Welcome House, there are several job coaches

If you need support in finding employment, you can meet with a job coach.

Job coaches support you on your journey into the job market.

Job coaches have contacts with employers across the entire job market in all industries.

What does a job coach do?

1. Discuss your background and preferences with you

2. Prepare you for a job interview

3. Contact employers and accompany you to the interview

Job coaches offer various group activities for you:


  • CV workshop
  • Interview training
  • Workshop: Working in Sweden
  • Information meetings about job industry in Sweden
  • Recruitment events

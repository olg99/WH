What does an educational and career advisor do?

Educational and career advisors help you plan your education and career.

Educational and career advisors:

  • provide information about different education and career paths and the requirements for entry
  • offer advice on how to apply for education programs and the necessary documents
  • assist you in making career plans and exploring various career opportunities
  • provide support and advice on various questions related to education, work, and career

Want to book a meeting with an educational and career advisor?

Ask one of us, and we will assist you!

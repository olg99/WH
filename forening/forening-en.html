What is an association?

An association is a group of people who share an idea or interest.

It can be any activity that you want to do together.

For example, it can be playing football, sewing clothes, cooking, singing, playing music, writing poems, going on excursions...

It can be almost anything.

Associations are important in Sweden

Most people living in Sweden are part of some association.

Being part of an association is both fun and important.


An association activity can help you:

  • strengthen your Swedish language skills – by speaking Swedish in a relaxed environment
  • improve your health – through physical activity or social interaction
  • build a new network – by getting to know new people in a new context

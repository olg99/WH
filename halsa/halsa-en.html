What is well-being?

Well-being means feeling good and being happy with life.

It can depend on many things, such as being healthy and having a good occupation, having good relationships with other people, and feeling safe and content.

Being well

It is important to take care of oneself and one's well-being to live a good and happy life.

Well-being is influenced by various factors:

  • food
  • exercise
  • sleep
  • managing stress
  • having support from friends and family
  • thinking positively

Get support and advice

At Welcome House, we offer various activities to help you improve your well-being.

You can also meet health informants who provide guidance and helpful advice.

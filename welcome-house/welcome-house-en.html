About Welcome House

Welcome to the City of Stockholm and Welcome House

If you are a newcomer and reside in the City of Stockholm, we can provide support during your initial period in Stockholm.

Welcome House hosts various activities and offers support that can be of assistance to you.

Who is Welcome House for:

  • You have a residence permit
  • It has been a maximum of 36 months since you received your residence permit
  • You are a refugee or have been granted subsidiary protection status
  • You live in the City of Stockholm
  • You are 18-65 years old

Welcome to visit us

Welcome to our drop-in! You don't need to register; just come by.
Our staff will show you around and answer your questions. Together, we can find activities that suit you.
